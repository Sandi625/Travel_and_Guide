Baluran Savana National Park

The Natural Beauty of Baluran Savanna

Baluran Savanna National Park, located in Banyuwangi, Indonesia, is a renowned natural tourist destination. The park boasts vast savanna landscapes and is home to diverse wildlife, including endangered species. Visitors can experience the breathtaking beauty of the savanna and encounter various animals and bird species.

Wildlife and Nature Exploration

Baluran Savanna is a paradise for nature enthusiasts. The park is inhabited by wild buffalo, deer, monkeys, and a variety of bird species. Nature lovers can explore well-maintained trails, allowing them to observe wildlife in their natural habitat. The park's diverse ecosystem and unique flora make it a perfect destination for eco-tourism.

Adventure and Outdoor Activities

Adventure seekers can indulge in outdoor activities such as trekking, bird watching, and wildlife photography. Baluran Savanna National Park offers a range of exciting adventures for visitors of all ages. The park's natural beauty and serene atmosphere provide an unforgettable experience for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.
